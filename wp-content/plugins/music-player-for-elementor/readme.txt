Music Player For Elementor is a stylish audio player addon for Elementor. Promote your music with an easy to use and highly customizable mp3 player and audio player.

== Description ==

Music Player for Elementor is a professionally designed audio player for WordPress. Promote your music and discography in a stylish way, with a dedicated Elementor widget that is very flexible and easy to use. The audio player WordPress plugin is perfect for musicians, artists, record labels, recording studios, DJs, podcasters, digital product stores and anyone working in the music industry.

### EASY-TO-USE

No initial configuration is needed to use the audio player. Install the plugin, edit any page in Elementor and drag the music player widget anywhere on your page. You can add or remove songs to your playlist, customize the audio player images, add purchase links for your album, change the player layout or add individual promo links for each song.

[youtube https://youtu.be/6CagCkhVauI]

### PROFESSIONAL DESIGN

Music Player for Elementor is a professionally designed tool that will enhance the overall look of your website. 

== Installation ==
1. Install Music Player for Elementor like any other WordPress plugin. Please take a short look on the [plugin installation tutorial](https://wordpress.org/support/article/managing-plugins/).
2. After installing and activating the plugin, you can simply use the music player widget in Elementor. There, you can add your own mp3/audio files, customize the player images, colors and layout. All you have to do is to add the player anywhere on your page.
